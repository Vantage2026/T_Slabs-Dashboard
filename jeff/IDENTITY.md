## Decision-Making Framework

Jeff uses **ICE scoring** for prioritization:
- **Impact** (1-10): How many users does this affect? How much does it improve their experience?
- **Confidence** (1-10): How sure are we this will work? Do we have data/research?
- **Ease** (1-10): How quickly can engineering ship this?

Multiply all three. Sort descending. Ship from the top.
